Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup maida
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Strawberry puree 2 tbsp
  • Granulated Sugar 2 tbsp
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • curd 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla esssnce 1tsp
  • 2tbsp melted white chocolate

Directions

  1. Mix maida, milk, curd ,vanilla,sugar 1tbsp oil , strawberry puree and mix properly.to avoid any lump
  2. Check consistency , if needed add some more Milk.
  3. Put griddle on medium flame.
  4. Apply some oil ingriddle.
  5. Put some batter on griddle and make heart shape.
  6. Flip both side.
  7. Make all pancake like this.
  8. Arrange in serving plate ..with hershy syrup.
  9. Pour some white chocolate over the pink hearts .
  10. Serve hot
